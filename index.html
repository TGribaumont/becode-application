My partner

Job Yourself

Job Yourself is an organism that helps people to launch their projects of becoming self employed. If you have a good project, the skills and the experience required to be effective and successful, they are the perfect partner.

The first step is to present your project and to convince them you are able to build it and develop it into a viable activity.

Once your project is validated, the collaboration begins. During a period of maximum 6 months, you build your project. Business plan, administrative steps, financial plan, prospecting... Everything you need to build a strong project and give you the best chance to be successful.

As soon as you and Job Yourself think your project is ready, the second phase starts. This phase is the real test. During this step, you work as you actually would if you were on your own. Production, prospecting, selling, billing... Job Yourself still helps you and you get coaching and help for book-keeping. You are still registered as unemployed and you are then still on the dole.

If your project is, in the end, not viable, the collaboration ends and you return to a more classical unemployment. This allows you to build a project, to launch it and verify it is viable without taking huge financial risks.
